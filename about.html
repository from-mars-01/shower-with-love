Our Story

Shower with Love was founded in 2020 by a group of volunteers who wanted to make a difference in the lives of those experiencing homelessness. We started with a single van and a mission to provide free showers, clean clothes, and hygiene products to those in need. Over the years, we have expanded our services and now operate multiple vans across the city, serving hundreds of individuals each month. Our goal is to restore dignity and hope to those we serve, one shower at a time.

Our team is made up of dedicated volunteers who are passionate about making a positive impact in our community. We believe that everyone deserves access to basic hygiene and the opportunity to feel clean and refreshed. Through our services, we aim to not only provide physical cleanliness but also to foster a sense of community and support for those who are often overlooked.

We are grateful for the support of our donors and volunteers who make our work possible. Together, we can continue to expand our reach and provide essential services to those in need. Thank you for being a part of our story and helping us make a difference in the lives of so many.
