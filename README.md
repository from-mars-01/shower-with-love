# Shower with Love Non Profit Website

## Mission statement
Shower with Love is an organization dedicated to helping unhoused people feel the dignity they deserve by
providing access to showers, haircuts, and clothing laundering services. 

## Website Purpose
The goal of this site is to provide information about our purpose and raise awareness of the ever growing issues of poverty and the struggles that unhoused people face every day of their lives. Through our site we accept donations, create opporutuintes for volunteers, and spread positivity.

## Target Audience
Our target audience would be people who are able to contribute in anywat possible but to also allow a platform for those who are in need to find information and get help if needed.
